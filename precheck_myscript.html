Prior Authorization Suite for Providers

PreCheck MyScript is designed for physicians and office administrative staff to run prescription trial claims and to initiate prescription prior authorizations.  Delivering real-time, accurate and patient-specific data, including out-of-pocket prescription costs for patients, PreCheck MyScripts provides lower-cost alternatives to save patients money and to avoid problems at the pharmacy, and alerts when a proposed prescription requires a prior authorization.

PreCheck MyScript works seamlessly into the physicians workflow as it is integrated during the point of prescription while the patient is in the office.  This reduces both phone calls and faxes, and helps to eliminate patient frustration and delays at the pharmacy when a prior authorization is needed.

Discovery

A Fusion session is a two day design discovery exercise where all members of the project are invited, including: UI/UX, project management, development leads, subject matter experts, and business & product owners.

We walk through a journey map siting frustrations, emotions, capabilities, and possible solutions. The information gathered with this session is then documented by the UX team and used in the next phase of design: Define.

FUSION

Define

Following the fusion session, we began interviewing physicians, administrative staff, and prior authorization subject matter experts to further our understanding of current concerns and recognize the areas in need of solutioning.  

Our deliverable included user flows of current processes and pain points and 3 possible levels of integration for the PreCheck MyScript application.

Design

Our UI style was derived from the UXDS Link Platform, but was altered to accommodate our Web Accessibility endeavor.  Color contrast and screen readability both aided in the design adaptation from the original Link styling to the PreCheck MyScript final look and feel.

We used Axure and its widget library to create a full UI Library.  This was used in creating our final compositions and interactive prototype.
