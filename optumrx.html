PROJECT OVERVIEW

OptumRx

Mobile App

RESPONSIBILITIES

UI design, Usability test prototype

OptumRx, one of the leading pharmacy benefit managers in the world.  But like much of the health care industry, OptumRx was behind in the efficiency of its product.  They looked to UXDS to design a new mobile application to help reduce prescription transaction times, increase prescription adherence, and to increase customer satisfaction.

UXDS adapted the existing web portal into a hybrid and responsive mobile application.  We also cut the average transaction time to refill or renew a prescription from about five minutes to 30 seconds or less.
