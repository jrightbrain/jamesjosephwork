Harken Health is an UnitedHealthcare driven initiative to create a new personalized care experience.  Newly designed neighborhood-based Health Centers were created to put remarkable care within more people’s reach.  With a foundation of listening and caring, Harken members are assigned health coaches to help set life-changing and health management goals.  Leading with kindness and holding themselves accountable to a much higher standard, Harken aims to make people feel truly cared for.  Harken proudly and passionately contributes to the connection between people’s lives and their overall health.
