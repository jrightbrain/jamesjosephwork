Point of Prescribing Solution Suite for Providers

PreCheck Myscript makes it easy to get:

Real-time, accurate, patient-specific data, including

out-of-pocket prescription costs for patients at their

selected pharmacy.


Information on preffered prescription alternatives to save patients money and avoid "sticker shock" at the pharmacy.
