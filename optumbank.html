Receipt Vault is a way for those with Optum Bank HSAs to track health care spending digitally, allowing users to reimburse themselves for out-of-pocket health care expenses years later. Receipt Vault stores and organizes images, leaving a digital trail of medical expenses for the IRS. With Receipt Vault, HSAs become a powerful investing tool for people who are seeking another vehicle outside of their 401k or IRAs.


Receipt Vault users can document an entire family’s qualified medical expenses easily. The tool allows users to upload images of health care transactions – receipts, bills, EOBs, credit card statements and more – make notes about each expense and input the eligilbe amount for reimbursement.
